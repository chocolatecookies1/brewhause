Our Story

Rooted in passion,
brewed with care.

Brewhaus was born from a single obsession: making coffee that actually means something. We work directly with small-scale farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — paying fair prices and building lasting relationships.

Every batch is roasted in-house, dialled in by our head roaster Priya, who left a corporate career to pursue the one thing that made mornings worth waking up for.

How We Do It

From farm to cup

01

Source

We partner with farms at altitude, selecting only the top 5% of each harvest. Every origin is visited in person.

02

Roast

Small-batch roasting on our vintage drum roaster, dialled in fresh every morning for peak flavour development.

03

Dial In

Every grind and extraction is calibrated to the gram. Our baristas are trained to obsessive levels of precision.

04

Serve

Poured with intention and served with warmth. We believe coffee is a conversation, not a transaction.
